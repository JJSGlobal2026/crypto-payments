Our Solutions
Three Crypto Payment Processing
Solutions for Every Business
No matter your comfort level with cryptocurrency, we have a crypto payment gateway built for your business model. Accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, and more — choose the path that fits, or combine them.
Solution 01
🔄
Crypto to Fiat
Zero Volatility Risk

How It Works

Your customer pays at checkout using cryptocurrency from their digital wallet. The moment the transaction is accepted, we instantly convert the crypto into fiat currency (USD) and settle it to your account. You never hold crypto — you receive dollars.

  • Customer pays in any supported cryptocurrency
  • Instant conversion to fiat at point of purchase
  • Merchant is completely shielded from price volatility
  • Settlement in USD to your existing bank account
  • No crypto wallet or exchange account needed
  • Simple plugin for WooCommerce, Magento & more — or full API integration
Ideal for: Merchants who want to accept crypto payments from customers but prefer to receive and hold only traditional fiat currency. The safest entry point into digital payments.
Solution 02
🪙
Crypto to Crypto
Instant Settlement

How It Works

Your customer pays with cryptocurrency and you accept that same cryptocurrency on a like-for-like basis. Funds settle instantly to your merchant wallet on our exchange. No conversion, no delay — pure crypto-native commerce.

  • Like-for-like cryptocurrency acceptance (BTC for BTC, ETH for ETH)
  • Instant settlement to your wallet on our exchange
  • Hold, trade, or convert on your own timeline
  • Full control over your crypto treasury strategy
  • Ideal for building long-term digital asset holdings
  • Integrates via simple eCommerce plugin or API
Ideal for: Crypto-forward merchants who are comfortable holding digital assets and want to build a position in cryptocurrency as part of their business strategy.
Solution 03
💳
Credit Card to Crypto
On-Ramp Solution
Decentralized

How It Works

At checkout, your customer uses their credit card to purchase a stablecoin (USDC). An on-ramp provider handles the crypto purchase, and the transaction completes. You receive funds virtually instantly in your non-custodial Polygon wallet. Fully decentralized, yet familiar for the customer.

  • Customer pays with a standard credit card
  • Stablecoin (USDC) is on-ramped at checkout
  • Funds land in your non-custodial Polygon wallet
  • Fully decentralized — operates outside traditional payment rails
  • Near-instant settlement with stablecoin price stability
  • Easy plugin setup for WooCommerce, Magento & other platforms
Ideal for: Merchants who want to operate on decentralized infrastructure while still giving their customers the familiarity of paying with a credit card. The bridge between traditional and decentralized finance.

FAQ
Common Questions About
Crypto Payment Processing

Do I need to hold or understand cryptocurrency to accept it?

No. With our Crypto to Fiat solution, you never touch cryptocurrency. Your customer pays in Bitcoin, Ethereum, or another supported coin — and you receive USD directly into your existing bank account. JJS Global handles the conversion instantly at the point of sale.

Does JJS Global's crypto payment gateway work with WooCommerce and Magento?

Yes. All three crypto payment solutions integrate with WooCommerce and Magento via a simple plugin install. For custom-built platforms or complex checkout flows, a full RESTful API integration is also available — giving your dev team complete control.

How fast does crypto payment settlement happen?

Settlement is instant or near-instant across all three solutions. Crypto to Fiat converts and settles to USD in real time at the moment of purchase. Crypto to Crypto settles directly to your exchange wallet instantly. The Credit Card On-Ramp delivers USDC stablecoin to your Polygon wallet near-instantly — no waiting 2–5 business days like traditional card processing.

What cryptocurrencies can my customers pay with?

JJS Global supports major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and other widely-used digital assets. The Credit Card On-Ramp solution uses USDC, a USD-pegged stablecoin, for price-stable settlement on the Polygon network. Contact our team for the full list of supported currencies.

Can crypto payments eliminate chargebacks for my business?

Yes. Cryptocurrency transactions are final and irreversible on the blockchain. Unlike credit card payments, crypto transactions cannot be disputed or reversed by the customer after the fact — eliminating chargeback fraud entirely. This is a significant advantage for high-risk merchants and businesses that regularly deal with disputed charges.

Is JJS Global's crypto payment processing available internationally?

Yes. Cryptocurrency operates without geographic borders, making it ideal for cross-border commerce. Merchants can accept payments from customers in any country without currency conversion headaches, international banking friction, or inflated cross-border processing fees. JJS Global serves both domestic and international merchants.
